Good News: ESC Certification

Asian Computer College (ACC) is now an ESC certified school! This means we meet the standards set by the Education Service Contracting (ESC) program and are eligible to receive government education subsidies for qualified students.

Scholarship Grants — APPLY NOW

Scholarship grants through government subsidy are available to incoming Grade 7 students. Interested applicants should contact the school office for requirements and deadlines. Limited slots — apply as soon as possible!

How to Apply for Scholarship / Enrollment

  1. Prepare application documents (birth certificate, grades, 2x2 ID photos, proof of residence).
  2. Visit or contact the school office for scholarship screening and requirements.
  3. Submit completed application before the announced deadline.
